**Who Cohere writes for:** large regulated enterprises (finance, government, healthcare, telecom) with
a GenAI mandate but a hard data constraint. The reader is an AI/ML or data-science lead who champions
the project, and behind them a security and compliance function that has to clear it. They are serious,
risk-aware, and allergic to hype and to anything that sounds like it treats their data casually.

**What they care about when they read Cohere:** that they stay in control of their data, that it will
pass security review, and that the vendor understands enterprise reality rather than selling a
frontier-race story. Write to a person who wants ownership and proof, not spectacle.
