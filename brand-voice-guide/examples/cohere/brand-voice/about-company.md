# About: Cohere

**What it is (plain):** enterprise AI, large language models, embeddings and retrieval, and North (an
agent platform), for companies with sensitive or regulated data. Toronto-based.

**The stance:** AI should be owned and controlled by the enterprise, deployed on their own data and
infrastructure, never at the cost of privacy. "We bring AI to your data," not the other way around.

**Proudest specifics:** enterprise-only since 2019, no consumer product and no chase for viral
moments. The value proposition is control: models are not trained on customer data, with private and
sovereign deployment. *"Why leading teams trust Cohere."*
