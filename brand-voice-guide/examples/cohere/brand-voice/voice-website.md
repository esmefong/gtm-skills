# Voice: Website: Cohere

Extends `voice-core.md`. For the marketing site and product pages (the primary intentional surface
harvested).

## 1. Structure
A short possessive-contrast or triad header, then a plain line that says the capability or the proof.
Ownership leads; the capability supports.

## 2. Hook patterns we use
- **The possessive contrast.** Sample: *"Own your AI."* / *"Our models. Your business."*
- **The triad.** Sample: *"Safe. Flexible. Independent."*
- **The trust frame.** Sample: *"Why leading teams trust Cohere."*

## 3. Hook patterns we avoid
- Model-race comparisons, benchmark bragging, "the most powerful X," anything that centers the model
  over the customer's control.

## 4. Format
- Short, imperative CTAs ("Go North," "Contact us," "Let's go"). Plain words carrying serious ideas.
  No hype, no exclamation-heavy energy.
