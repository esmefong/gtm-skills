# Writing Standard: Cohere

The concrete rules. Apply after the voice rules in `voice-core.md`.

## Do
- **Words we use:** *own, your, data, infrastructure, sovereign, private, secure, control, trust,
  deploy, bring the model to your data.* Sample: *"Your data. Your infrastructure. Cohere keeps it
  that way."*
- **Address:** always second person, *your / you*. The signature is the possessive contrast, *your*
  vs *our* ("Our models. Your business.").
- **Structure:** short fragments and triads ("Safe. Flexible. Independent."). Imperative CTAs ("Go
  North," "Let's go," "Contact us").
- **Capitalization:** sentence case, clean and serious.

## Avoid  *(built last)*
- **Banned words:** *revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, unleash, supercharge,* and
  frontier-race superlatives generally.
- **HIGH: Hype or frontier-race framing** ("the most powerful model ever," benchmark bragging).
  Breaks the calm, enterprise-serious pillar. Instead: state the control and capability plainly.
- **HIGH: Burying the customer's control.** Copy that centers "our model's power" instead of "your
  data, your control." Instead: make "your" the point (the distinctiveness test).
- **MEDIUM: Jargon without a plain frame.** Instead: pair any technical term with a plain word
  ("safe," "trust," "control").
- **MEDIUM: Third person** ("enterprises can…"). Instead: "You can…"
- **LOW: Long, hedged sentences.** Instead: cut to a short declarative or a triad.
