# Brand voice directory: Cohere

> **Real company, public data.** Extracted from Cohere's **public** marketing copy (see
> [`../input.md`](../input.md) for the harvest and sources). Samples are verbatim public copy. This is
> a demonstration of the `brand-voice-guide` skill's output, not an official Cohere document.

This is the primary worked example, and it pairs with the Cohere ICP from `icp-research` to show the
full system on one company: who Cohere sells to, then how Cohere sounds. It is a distinct voice from
the repo's secondary example: **enterprise-sovereign and ownership-framed**, where Notion is warm and
playful.

| File | What it holds |
|---|---|
| `voice-core.md` | The character: tonal pillars (each with a verbatim sample), sentence rules, persona-we-are-NOT, the distinctiveness test |
| `writing-standard.md` | The concrete rules: a *Do* half and a severity-tagged *Avoid* half |
| `voice-website.md` | The primary intentional surface harvested (marketing site and product pages) |
| `about-company.md` | Light voice context: what it is, the stance |
| `icp.md` | Light reader context, pointing to the full Cohere ICP from `icp-research` |
| `real-examples.md` | In-voice lines to reference for shape |

## Gaps
- Harvest is marketing-surface only. Cohere's blog, research papers, and enterprise sales collateral
  have their own registers not captured here. *Next:* harvest the blog and add `voice-blog.md`.
- No first-party input. Pillars are inferred from public copy, not confirmed by the team.
