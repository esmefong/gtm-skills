# Real Examples: Cohere

In-voice lines to reference for *shape*, not to copy.

- **Possessive contrast:** *"Your data. Your infrastructure. Cohere keeps it that way."* / *"Our
  models. Your business."*
- **Triad:** *"Safe. Flexible. Independent."*
- **Ownership imperative:** *"Own your AI."*
- **Trust frame:** *"Why leading teams trust Cohere."*

## What to learn from these
Make "your" the point; land the rhythm in short fragments or a triad; carry a serious idea in plain
words; stay calm and confident, never in a model-race brag.
