## Observed patterns (analyst notes, not brand copy)
- The signature move is a **possessive contrast**: "Your data… Cohere keeps it that way." / "Our
  models. Your business." The customer owns; Cohere enables.
- Short declarative fragments, often in **triads**: "Safe. Flexible. Independent."
- Second person throughout ("your," "you"), plus imperative verbs ("Own," "Go North," "Let's go").
- Plain words ("safe," "flexible," "trust") carrying enterprise-serious ideas, not jargon or hype.
- Confident and calm. No frontier-race bravado, no "revolutionary," no viral-moment energy.
