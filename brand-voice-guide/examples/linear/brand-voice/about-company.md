# About — Linear

**What it is (plain):** software for planning and building products — issue tracking, projects, and
product operations for engineering teams (and, now, their AI agents).

**The stance / contrarian belief:** a work tool should be *purpose-built and fast*, not an infinitely
configurable everything-app. Speed and taste are the product, not a feature.

**Proudest specifics:** a reputation for the fastest, most crafted tool in its category. *"Made for
modern software teams."*
