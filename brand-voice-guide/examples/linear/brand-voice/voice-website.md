# Voice — Website — Linear

Extends `voice-core.md`. For the marketing site and product pages (the primary intentional surface
harvested).

## 1. Structure
A short declarative header ("Understand progress at scale") + one plain benefit sentence beneath it.
Capability becomes the headline; the sentence says why it matters.

## 2. Hook patterns we use
- **The *purpose-built* framing.** Sample: *"Purpose-built for planning and building products."*
- **The two-beat.** Sample: *"Built for the future. Available today."*
- **Capability-as-header.** Sample: *"Make product operations self-driving."*

## 3. Hook patterns we avoid
- Greetings, questions-as-headlines, "Learn more," anything that could headline a generic PM tool.

## 4. Format
- Single-verb CTAs with an arrow. No emoji. Minimal, high-contrast copy.
