**Who Linear writes for:** software teams that feel their current tool taxing their focus — engineers,
engineering managers, and product leaders who value speed, craft, and getting the tool out of the way.
Technically literate; allergic to hype and to bloated enterprise software.

**What they care about when they read Linear:** that this is built by people who understand how they
actually work (PRDs to PRs), that it will be fast, and that it won't make them sit through a sales
voice. Write to the person who would rather see one true sentence than three adjectives.
