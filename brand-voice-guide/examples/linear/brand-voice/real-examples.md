# Real Examples — Linear

In-voice lines to reference for *shape*, not to copy.

- **Header + benefit:** *"Understand progress at scale."* → *"…dashboards that surface what needs your
  attention."*
- **Two-beat close:** *"Built for the future. Available today."*
- **CTA set:** *Intake → · Plan → · Build → · Diffs → · Monitor →*

## What to learn from these
Open on the statement; one idea per line; the benefit is always momentum; the plain sentence carries
the weight, so no exclamation is ever needed.
