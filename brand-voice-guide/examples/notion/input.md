## Observed patterns (analyst notes, not brand copy)
- Pithy two-beat contrasts: "Less tracking. More progress." / "One workspace. Every team."
- Direct second-person address: "You assign the tasks…", "…built for your team."
- Warm, plain, benefit-first; small moments of play (the capitalized "Think", the em-friendly rhythm).
- Superlatives kept simple and human ("Perfect notes, every time") rather than technical.
- Ampersands and casual connectors ("docs & projects") over formal phrasing.
