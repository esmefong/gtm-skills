# About — Notion

**What it is (plain):** one connected workspace where a team's notes, docs, projects, and knowledge
live together — now with AI and agents built in.

**The stance:** work tools shouldn't be cold or siloed; everything belongs in one friendly place a
person actually wants to open.

**Proudest specifics:** the tool teams reach for to replace a stack of scattered apps. *"One workspace.
Every team."*
