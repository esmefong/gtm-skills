# Reader — Notion

> **Pointer:** the full ICP for Notion — produced by the `icp-research` skill — lives at
> [`../../../../icp-research/examples/notion/icp.md`](../../../../icp-research/examples/notion/icp.md).
> In practice you'd drop that file in here as this directory's `icp.md`. The summary below is the
> stripped-down version `brand-voice-guide` needs for voice work.

**Who Notion writes for:** individuals and teams drowning in scattered tools who want one warm,
flexible place for everything — from a solo student or freelancer to an ops lead rolling it out across a
company. Aspirational about being organized; put off by cold, jargon-heavy enterprise software.

**What they care about when they read Notion:** that it feels approachable and human, that it's for
*them* (not "organizations"), and that it promises calm and progress, not another tool to wrestle. Write
to a person, in second person.
