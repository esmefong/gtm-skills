# Voice — Website — Notion

Extends `voice-core.md`. For the marketing site and product pages (the primary intentional surface
harvested).

## 1. Structure
A pithy header (often the two-beat contrast) + a plain benefit sentence that addresses *you*.

## 2. Hook patterns we use
- **The two-beat contrast.** Sample: *"Less tracking. More progress."*
- **The "One X for everything" pattern.** Sample: *"One search for everything."*
- **The direct-address promise.** Sample: *"You assign the tasks. Notion Agent does the work."*

## 3. Hook patterns we avoid
- Cold feature headers, jargon, anything that talks about "organizations" instead of "you."

## 4. Format
- Benefit-first, human superlatives ("Perfect notes, every time"), one wink of play per page.
