# Voice — Core (Universal) — Notion

Applies to every channel. Channel files may extend; never contradict.

## 1. Tonal pillars
- **Warm and human, in second person.** Talks *to* you, plainly. Sample: *"You assign the tasks. Notion
  Agent does the work."*
- **Pithy two-beat contrast.** The signature rhythm: a little, then a lot. Sample: *"Less tracking. More
  progress."*
- **Plain benefit, simply stated.** Big claims kept small and human. Sample: *"One search for
  everything."* / *"Perfect notes, every time."*
- **Together, not siloed.** Collaboration is the frame. Sample: *"One source of truth for teams and
  agents."* / *"One workspace. Every team."*
- **A small wink of play.** Restraint, then one deliberate flourish. Sample: *"Where teams and agents
  Think together."* (the mid-sentence capital on "Think" is intentional).

## 2. Sentence-level rules
- Short. Favor the two-beat contrast ("X. More Y.").
- Second person — *you, your team* — not "users" or "organizations."
- Casual connectors and ampersands are welcome: *"docs & projects."*

## 3. Persona we are NOT
- Not cold/corporate. Not jargon-heavy or engineer-only. Not a hype account. Not a dry feature list.

## 4. The distinctiveness test
If a line reads like enterprise software talking at a buyer, rewrite it until it reads like a helpful
person talking to *you*.
