# Real Examples — Notion

In-voice lines to reference for *shape*, not to copy.

- **Two-beat contrast:** *"Less tracking. More progress."*
- **The "one X" promise:** *"One search for everything."* / *"One workspace. Every team."*
- **Direct-address benefit:** *"You assign the tasks. Notion Agent does the work."*

## What to learn from these
Talk to "you"; keep the claim plain and warm; land the rhythm in two beats; allow exactly one small
wink.
